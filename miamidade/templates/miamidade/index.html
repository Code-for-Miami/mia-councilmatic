Miami-Dade County Commission consists of 13 elected commissioners and they meet monthly to shape the county. The commission works with Carlos A. Gimenez, the Mayor, and Harvey Ruvin, the County Clerk.

Miami Councilmatic tracks all things related to Miami-Dade County Commission:

  • the legislation introduced and passed
  • its various committees and the meetings they hold
  • the commissioners themselves

One of the more unusual aspects of Miami-Dade Commission is the volume of ordinances that are introduced and passed every month. Most of these are routine items like sign permits, damaged vehicle claims, sidewalk cafe approvals and honorifics.

To help sort through all of this, we classify all legislation and tag it appropriately. If something doesn’t look like a routine piece of legislation, we tag it as Non-Routine to make it easier to discover.
