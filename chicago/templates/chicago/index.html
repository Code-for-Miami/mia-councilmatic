Miami-Dade County Council consists of 13 elected commissioners and they meet monthly to shape the county. The council works with Carlos A. Gimenez, the Mayor, and Harvey Ruvin, the County Clerk.

Miami Councilmatic tracks all things related to Miami-Dade County Council:

  • the legislation introduced and passed
  • its various committees and the meetings they hold
  • the commissioners themselves
